Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

This peanut butter banana smoothie is so refreshing, and it's sweet and tasty.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • Half cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
  3. Enjoy!!
